Where leaders go to discover the latest trends Conferences

Conferences are a great place to learn about the industry, meet people, uncover trends, and talk with security vendors.

InfoSec Conferences is a great resource for Security Conferences all over the world. Below is a list of some of the highlights.

https://infosec-conferences.com/

Volunteer

Many of these conferences, specifically the ones without vendors, LOVE to have volunteers. This is a great way to give back, serve, meet new people, and learn.

TRADE ASSOCIATIONS

Trade organizations like ISSA have local chapter meetings near where you live or work. You can look them up and see when they meet. The Chapters usually have dues each year to cover basic operational costs for local events as well as food for meetings.

CSA Mission Statement

Our mission is to enhance student learning and professional growth by providing opportunities for students to better prepare themselves academically and professionally. We accomplish this through competitions, learning activities, special guest speakers, student and alumni networking, and providing groups to study for cyber security related certifications.
