CSA – 1: Foundation: Operating System (OS)

In this LinkedIn Learning Collection, you will learn about VMs (Virtual Machines), how to set up Kali Linux, the overview of some of the top Kali tools, Debian Linux, Bash, Linux Command Line, SSH and more.

Estimated time: 8 Hours.  Practice on picoCTF & Overthewire

Link: Foundations Collection

CSA – 2: Recon & Scanning

In this LinkedIn Learning Collection, you will learn about recon, planning & scoping, vulnerability analysis and scans.  This is a first step when determining what you have in your environment.  Once you know your target machines, you can focus on finding vulnerabilities in each one.  This will prepare you to identify which machines need better protection.

Estimated time: 16 Hours.  Practice on http://scanme.nmap.org/

Link: Recon & Scanning Collection

CSA Mission Statement

Our mission is to enhance student learning and professional growth by providing opportunities for students to better prepare themselves academically and professionally. We accomplish this through competitions, learning activities, special guest speakers, student and alumni networking, and providing groups to study for cyber security related certifications.
